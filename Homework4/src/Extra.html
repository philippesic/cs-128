WURFL

Wurfl is a device detection and optimization database that helps websites and applications deliver the best user experience on any device. It provides accurate information about the capabilities and features of the device accessing the website, allowing for dynamic optimization of content and functionality.

What can Wurfl do?

  • WURFL can detect the screen size of a requesting device, allowing developers to optimize the layout and content of their application for the specific device.

  • WURFL can detect the image formats that the requesting device can display, allowing developers to optimize image formats for different devices to minimize load times and improve performance.

  • WURFL can detect the operating system running on the requesting device, allowing developers to optimize their application's behavior for different operating systems, such as iOS or Android.
